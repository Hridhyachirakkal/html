Sachin Ramesh Tendulkar is an Indian former international cricketer who captained the Indian national team. He is widely regarded as one of the greatest batsmen in the history of cricket.

Born: 24 April 1973 (age 51 years), Mumbai Spouse: Anjali Tendulkar (m. 1995) Children: Sara Tendulkar, Arjun Tendulkar Dates joined: 2008 (Mumbai Indians), MORE Awards: Bharat Ratna, Padma Vibhushan, Padma Shri, MORE Height: 1.65 m Parents: Rajni Tendulkar, Ramesh Tendulkar

Achievements

  • 1994: Arjuna Award recipient for achievements in cricket.
  • 1997: Tendulkar was one of the five cricketers selected as Wisden Cricketer of the Year.
  • 1997/98: India's highest sporting honour – Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna.
  • 1999: Padma Shri – India's fourth highest civilian award.
